All about Kristen

Kristen Jaymes Stewart was born to Jules Mann and John Stewart in Los Angeles, California. She also has an older brother, Cameron. Her father was a stage manager and television producer and her mother was a script supervisor. Her family transferred to Colorado, where they lived for several years before they moved back to Los Angeles. At the age of eight, she was noticed by a talent scout agent during a Christmas play, who offered her a non-speaking role in a Disney TV movie titled, ‘The Thirteenth Year’. Thus, her tryst with acting began.


Her prolific acting skills from a very young age went on to inspire a number of young girls and teenagers alike. She was spotted by a talent scout and despite landing a small role in the Disney series, ‘The Thirteenth Year’, she made her presence felt with a stunning, non-verbal performance.

Kristen's Achievements

  • In 2009 She won a Teen Choice Award for ‘Choice Movie Actress: Drama’ for ‘Twilight’.
  • In 2010 She was honored with an Orange Rising Star Award by the British Academy Film Association.
  • She won an MTV Movie Award for ‘Best Female Performance’ for ‘The Twilight Saga: Eclipse’, in 2011.
  • She won a People’s Choice Award for ‘Favorite Face of Heroism’ for ‘Snow White and the Huntsman’, in 2013.
